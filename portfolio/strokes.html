Stroke - Freestyle

This should already be familiar to you, pretty much the easiest stroke of the bunch. The basics here are you pull with one arm at a time in rotation while you kick up and down with each leg independently or flutter kick

  • Strokes are done by pulling with one arm at a time

Stoke - Backstroke

This is also a pretty common stroke, very similar to free except you're on your back. The general idea is similar to free, except you want to feel a lot more power in your pull as you'll end up pushing the water behind you. Make sure you keep your neck and head flat, you'll slow down a lot if you try to lift them up.

Stroke - Breastroke

Now this is the first tricky one we get to, the best way I can describe it is that it looks like frog in the water. You're going to pull with both arms apart
